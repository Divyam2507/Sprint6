Home Loan - Benefits and Features

A Home is one of the biggest purchases in an individual's life. To buy a home without securing a home loan is tough financially for most people. We, at IBS Bank, understand the needs of our customers and partner with them so that they can become proud home owners on terms that are fair and simple. With a IBS Bank Home Loan, you can buy a flat or a house without having to worry about putting together a huge down payment.

        

Key Benefits:

  • Simple and hassle free loan processing with minimum turn-around time.
  • Loans up to 90% of cost of property based on eligibility.
  • Term Loan secured against residential properties
